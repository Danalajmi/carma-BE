# ðŸš— Carma

**Carma** is a web application designed for moments when your car breaks down and you just want to quickly find the *right* repair shop. It connects car owners with suitable garages based on car model and service needs, making the repair process faster and stress-free.

---

## ðŸ“Œ Project Overview

Carma helps users:
- Manage their cars
- Request repair services
- Automatically find garages that can handle their specific car model and service requirements
- View trusted reviews pulled from Google Maps
- Add their own reviews to share experiences

Garage owners can manage their garages and the services they offer, while the system handles matching requests to the most suitable garages.
